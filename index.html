Smart Irrigation System

Precision Farming for the Future

About the Project

This project demonstrates a Smart Irrigation System designed to optimize water usage in precision farming, ensuring crops receive the right amount of water at the right time.

Features

  • Automated water distribution based on soil moisture levels.
  • Real-time data monitoring.
  • Mobile app integration for remote control.

Meet the Team

We are a group of college students passionate about sustainable farming and technology.
